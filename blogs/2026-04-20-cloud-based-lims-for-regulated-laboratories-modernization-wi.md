<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-what-is-samplemanager-lims-cloud-hosted">What is SampleManager LIMS cloud-hosted?</h2>

Unlike traditional on-premises systems, it removes the burden of maintaining infrastructure, allowing laboratories to focus on scientific outcomes rather than IT operations. <h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-why-regulated-laboratories-need-more-than-saas">Why regulated laboratories need more than SaaS</h2>



<p>While SaaS LIMS solutions prioritize speed and standardization, regulated laboratories often require flexibility and control. Validation processes, complex workflows, and integration with instruments or enterprise systems demand a more adaptable approach.<br /><a href="https://www.thermofisher.com/us/en/home/digital-solutions/lab-informatics/lab-information-management-systems-lims/solutions/samplemanager.html?cid=E.26CMD.DS102.37508.01">SampleManager LIMS</a> cloud hosted enables organizations to adopt cloud infrastructure without sacrificing validation rigor or integration depth. This is particularly important in environments governed by GxP, QA/QC, or ISO standards, where system changes must be carefully managed and documented.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-reducing-it-burden-while-maintaining-control">Reducing IT burden while maintaining control</h2>



<p>One of the key benefits of a cloud-hosted model is the ability to eliminate infrastructure complexity. Provisioning, monitoring, patching, and system maintenance are managed by the provider, allowing laboratory teams to focus on quality, compliance, and productivity. This approach combines the operational advantages of the cloud with the control traditionally associated with on-premises systems—delivering the best of both worlds.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-built-for-compliance-and-data-integrity">Built for compliance and data integrity</h2>



<p>In regulated environments, data security and auditability are non-negotiable. SampleManager LIMS cloud hosted supports these requirements through:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Role-based access control and multi-factor authentication</li>



<li>Encryption of data in transit and at rest</li>



<li>Continuous monitoring and logging for audit trails</li>



<li>Validated workflows aligned with regulatory expectations</li>
</ul>



<p>These capabilities help ensure that laboratory data remains secure, traceable, and compliant.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-scalability-and-resilience-for-global-operations">Scalability and resilience for global operations</h2>



<p>Cloud infrastructure enables laboratories to scale efficiently and support global operations. With access to distributed cloud resources, organizations can expand capacity, improve system availability, and recover quickly from disruptions. High availability configurations and automated recovery mechanisms further support business continuity, making cloud-hosted LIMS a reliable foundation for modern laboratory environments.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-a-balanced-approach-to-cloud-adoption">A balanced approach to cloud adoption</h2>



<p>Cloud adoption in regulated laboratories is not simply about moving systems off-premises—it’s about doing so without introducing risk. Cloud based LIMS provides a balanced approach by combining modern infrastructure with enterprise-grade control, security, and configurability. It allows laboratories to modernize at their own pace while maintaining alignment with regulatory and operational requirements.</p>
