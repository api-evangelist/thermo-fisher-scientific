How digitally mature is your laboratory today? Many organizations have already invested in laboratory automation, laboratory information management systems (LIMS), electronic laboratory notebooks (ELNs), and connected instruments. Yet despite these investments, scientists often continue to spend valuable time moving data between systems, reconciling results, and managing fragmented workflows.
