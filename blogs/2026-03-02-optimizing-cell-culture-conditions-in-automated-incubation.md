What are optimal cell culture conditions? Optimal cell culture conditions are environmental parameters that maintain cell viability, reproducibility, and biological integrity. For most mammalian cell cultures, this includes 37°C temperature, >90% relative humidity, and 4–10% CO₂ concentration.
