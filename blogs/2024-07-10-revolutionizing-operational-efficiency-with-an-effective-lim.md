<p><img alt="chemical manufacturing plant" class="size-full wp-image-3178 aligncenter" height="667" src="https://www.thermofisher.com/blog/connectedlab/wp-content/uploads/sites/14/2024/07/chemical-manufacturing-plant.jpg" width="1000" /></p>
<p>In chemical manufacturing, operational efficiency is pivotal. A key aspect of streamlining operations is the quality of process manufacturing, which is often challenged by outdated systems, unique operational requirements, and cybersecurity threats. A robust Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) can be a game-changing tool in this context. This blog post explores the transformative journey of a multinational chemical manufacturer that successfully enhanced its operational efficiency by adopting an innovative LIMS.</p>
<h4><strong>The need for a new solution</strong></h4>
<p>Like numerous global <a href="https://www.thermofisher.com/us/en/home/digital-solutions/lab-informatics/lims-chemical-petrochemical.html?cid=E.24CMD.DS119.23162.01">chemical and petrochemical</a> organizations, the company found itself grappling with substantial cybersecurity threats due to a heavily customized and unsupported LIMS. With system upgrades becoming increasingly complicated, the company realized the need for a solution that could not only mitigate these challenges but also enhance functionality and streamline workflows within the laboratories. The answer to these issues came in the form of <a href="https://www.thermofisher.com/us/en/home/digital-solutions/lab-informatics/samplemanager-lims.html?cid=E.24CMD.DS119.23162.01">Thermo Scientific</a><a href="https://www.thermofisher.com/us/en/home/digital-solutions/lab-informatics/samplemanager-lims.html?cid=E.24CMD.DS119.23162.01">™</a><a href="https://www.thermofisher.com/us/en/home/digital-solutions/lab-informatics/samplemanager-lims.html?cid=E.24CMD.DS119.23162.01"> SampleManager™ LIMS software</a>.</p>
<p>SampleManager LIMS, a comprehensive laboratory digital solution, is renowned for its high configurability, robust security, and reliability. The company aimed to implement this system across its 25 global facilities specializing in chemicals, polymers, and research and development. A mammoth task, but one that the company was ready to undertake to leverage advanced technology and enhance operations.</p>
<p><img alt="scientist working with machine" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3179" height="667" src="https://www.thermofisher.com/blog/connectedlab/wp-content/uploads/sites/14/2024/07/scientist-working-with-machine.jpg" width="1000" /></p>
<p>One of the crucial aspects of the implementation was the integration of SampleManager LIMS with <a href="https://www.thermofisher.com/order/catalog/product/CHROMELEON7">Thermo Scientific™ Chromeleon™ CDS software</a> and SAP, which were already in use within the company. This strategic move eliminated redundant data entry, leading to a significant boost in operational efficiency. Analytical results generated in the lab would now automatically reflect in the SAP system, significantly reducing the chances of human error and enhancing data accuracy.</p>
<p>The integration of SampleManager LIMS with Chromeleon CDS was a significant milestone. It not only eliminated manual data entry but also improved data integrity and streamlined laboratory workflows. The seamless integration led to increased efficiency in tasks like data searching and reporting, which could now be performed directly within the client and easily configured as required.</p>
<h4><strong>Better data analysis through standardized naming </strong></h4>
<p>The introduction of a standardized naming system across all sites allowed for easier and more effective data analysis. By maintaining consistent nomenclature, SampleManager LIMS fostered a dependable data management system. Furthermore, the incorporation of SampleManager LIMS prepared the organization for future advancements, offering a solid base for the integration of innovative technologies which could unlock unprecedented levels of lab operation efficiency and analytical depth.</p>
<h4><strong>Communications and resource allocation</strong></h4>
<p>Reflecting on the implementation process, the importance of clear and open communication became evident. Setting clear and realistic expectations about the project’s scope from the onset, understanding the level of effort required for the implementation, and efficiently allocating resources were critical to the project&#8217;s success. These important lessons can be applied by organizations worldwide seeking to improve their operations using software solutions.</p>
<p>The experience of this company provides a valuable blueprint for other businesses considering a similar transition. Despite the challenges, the effort was well worth it, leading to improved operational efficiency and productivity through streamlined and reliable processes.</p>
<p>The key to the successful implementation was the software and the partnership with Thermo Fisher Scientific. The company’s expansive knowledge in data mapping and system integration played a crucial role in overcoming challenges and ensuring project success. This illustrates that the right software and implementation partner can transform operations.</p>
